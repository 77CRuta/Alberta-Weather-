#### ğŸ” What the X-Axis Represents

| X-Axis Label | Full Station Name | Location |

A **weather instrument** in this context refers to the physical equipment installed at each station site. Each station typically includes a combination of:
- A **thermometer** (usually a platinum resistance thermometer housed in a ventilated Stevenson screen) to record daily maximum, minimum, and mean air temperature
- An **ultrasonic or acoustic snow depth sensor** to measure the depth of the snowpack on the ground
- A **precipitation gauge** (tipping bucket or weighing gauge) to capture total rainfall and snowfall accumulation
- A **data logger** that records and transmits readings to Environment Canada automatically

The **CS** (Climate Station) designation indicates instruments maintained to a higher standard of calibration and consistency, specifically for long-term climate trend monitoring rather than day-to-day weather forecasting.
